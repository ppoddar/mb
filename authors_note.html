Author's Note
Mahabharath by KrishDwapiyan Vyaas is one of the greatest ancient Indian texts. To read the epic in translation in entireity is difficult for common reader. To the curious, the entire epic is an invaluable treasure of ancient culture, no part of it is ignoreable. But for common reader wishing to read the essential narrative, many ancillaries are mere hinderances.

This website is a translation of gist of Mahabharatha. https://www.amazon.in/Mahabharat-Rajsekhar-Basu/dp/8171571549 It contains all chapters of the original and almost all sub-chapters; only parts such as detailed geneology, excessive description of war scences, political, relegious and philosophical discussions, hymns and oftrepeated parts and boring parts are smmarized or ommitted. The purpose of summarization is to maintain the main theme as far as possible while present the epic in an easy-to-read manner. Mahabharath is a classic, in the true sense of the word.
